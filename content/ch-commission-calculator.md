### Who Pays Taxes?

All residents and workers in Switzerland with income above the personal allowance/threshold must pay income tax. This includes:
- Employees (taxes withheld from paycheck)
- Self-employed individuals (quarterly or annual payments)
- Foreign workers with Swiss source income
- Residents of all cantons
